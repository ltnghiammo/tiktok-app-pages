Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-04

This Privacy Policy explains how the application "Itnghiammo" ("the App") handles information.

1. Who Uses the App

The App is a personal, single-user automation tool. It is used only by its developer/owner to publish the developer's own video content to the developer's own TikTok account. The App is not distributed to or used by other end users.

2. Information Collected

The App accesses only the OAuth access token issued by TikTok for the developer's own authenticated account, solely to upload and publish video files created by the developer. The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data belonging to other TikTok users, and it does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking data.

3. How Information Is Used

The TikTok access token is used exclusively to call TikTok's Content Posting API (video upload and publish endpoints) on behalf of the developer's own account. It is stored locally in the developer's private environment configuration and is never transmitted to any third party.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

The developer may revoke the App's access at any time from TikTok's account settings, which immediately invalidates the access token. No user data is retained by the App beyond what is required for active publishing.

5. Third-Party Services

The App communicates only with TikTok's official API endpoints (open.tiktokapis.com). No other third-party services receive data collected through this integration.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the app developer directly.
